Cookie

These cake mix cookies are yummy and simple to make with just 3 easy ingredients!

This recipe makes it easy for even those who has never tried baking before.

Ingredients

  • 1 (18.25 ounce) package white cake mix

  • one half cup vegetable oil

  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F

  2. Stir cake mix, oil and eggs together in a large owl until it comes together as dough

  3. Form dough into 1-inch balls and place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets

  4. Bake in the preheated oven , about 6 to 10 minutes, depending on how soft you like you cookies.COol cookies on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring the to a wire rack to cool completely.
